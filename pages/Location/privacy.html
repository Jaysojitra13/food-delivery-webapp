Privacy Policy

Thank you for choosing our food delivery website! We are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your personal information. Please read the following privacy policy to understand how we collect, use, and disclose your information:

Information We Collect:

When you use our website, we may collect personal information, such as your name, email address, phone number, and address. We may also collect information about your order history, payment information, and location.

How We Use Your Information:

We use your information to process your orders, communicate with you about your orders, and improve our services. We may also use your information to send you promotional emails or newsletters.

Disclosure of Your Information:

We may disclose your information to third-party service providers who help us provide our services, such as payment processors, delivery partners, and marketing agencies. We may also disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights or the rights of others.

Cookies:

We use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your user experience and collect information about your browsing behavior on our website.

Security:

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. However, we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

Changes to Orders:

If you need to make changes to your order, please contact us as soon as possible. We will do our best to accommodate your request, but we cannot guarantee that we will be able to make the changes.

Children's Privacy:

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 18. \ We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 18.

Changes to Privacy Policy:

We reserve the right to change this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on our website, and your continued use of our website will constitute acceptance of the new privacy policy.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please Contact us
